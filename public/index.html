What is Backcountry?

Backcountry is a hiking collaboration site that provides you with all the tools you need to plan your next hiking or backpacking adventure.

Build a trip

Click on (toolbar icon) to expand toolbar.
Click on "Create New Trip"to begin building a new trip.
Enter dates of trip, trip name, trailhead name and build your route on the map by searching for location and clicking on trail points to create marker points. When finished, click "Save Route"

Edit a trip

Only the creator of a trip has permission to archive, delete and edit trip details.
To edit trip details, click on (edit icon) under the trip you wish to edit.
and enter changes into the corresponding fields. To add trail points to route, click map to add markers. To clear route and start again, click on clear button. To save click "Save Changes"

Archive a trip

Click on (archive icon) under the trip you wish to archive.
This will remove the trip from your dashboard and the dashboards of collaborators.
To view your list of archived trips, expand the toolbar and click on "Archived Trips". From the archived page you can reactivate a trip by clicking on "Reactivate Trip".

Share a trip

Click on (share icon) under the trip you wish to share.
In the input field enter the name of the collaborator and select the correct person from the dropdown. Click "Share" to submit. Trip sharing allows the collaborator to see all trip details and build gear/food lists for themselves and others.
